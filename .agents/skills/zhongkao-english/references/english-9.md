# 九年级英语（上册 + 下册 + 中考总复习）

> 外研版（New Standard English）九年级英语教材知识点框架。待补充完整内容时，请参照 [textbook-guide.md](textbook-guide.md) 的格式添加。

## 上册

### Module 1: Wonders of the world
**核心语法**：特殊疑问词 + to do、语法复习综合
**重点句型**：
- That's news to me! I don't know what to say.
- Let's call Wonders of the World and join in the discussion.
- I've never seen it, so I'm not sure I agree with you.
- In my opinion, man-made wonders are more exciting than natural ones.

**核心词汇**：wonder, ancient, natural, man-made, discussion, opinion, in one's opinion, more than, on the eastern coast of

### Module 2: Public holidays
**核心语法**：定语从句（that/which 修饰物）
**重点句型**：
- It's a vacation that/which we celebrate in November.
- The food that/which people eat at Thanksgiving is traditional.
- I know a place which/that sells wonderful holiday cards.

**核心词汇**：celebrate, flag, season, vacation, among, until, public, start, depend on, count down, as well

### Module 3: Heroes
**核心语法**：定语从句（who 修饰人）
**重点句型**：
- He is a doctor who works for the Chinese government.
- The woman who lives next door is a nurse.
- Norman Bethune was a Canadian doctor who came to China in 1938.
- He's someone who I admire very much.

**核心词汇**：hero, attend, take care of, manage, whatever, give up, die for, treat, wounded, save

### Module 4: Home alone
**核心语法**：定语从句（综合：that/which/who/whose）
**重点句型**：
- He was a doctor whose patients were mainly children.
- Is there anything that I need to know?
- It's the worst thing that has ever happened to me.
- The person who answered the phone told me you were away.

**只能用 that 的情况**：
1. 先行词是 everything, something, anything, nothing 等不定代词
2. 先行词被 the only, the very, the first, the last 修饰
3. 先行词既有人又有物
4. 先行词被序数词或最高级修饰

### Module 5: Museums
**核心语法**：间接引语（宾语从句综合）
**重点句型**：
- He said (that) he would come tomorrow.
- She asked me if/whether I had ever been to the Science Museum.
- They told us (that) it was free to enter.
- He wanted to know when the museum opened.

**直接引语→间接引语变化**：
- 时态后推：am/is→was, are→were, do/does→did, will→would, can→could, have/has→had
- 代词变化：I→he/she, we→they, my→his/her
- 时间/地点变化：today→that day, tomorrow→the next day, here→there, this→that

### Module 6: Problems
**核心语法**：if 引导的条件状语从句复习、unless = if...not
**重点句型**：
- If I tell him the truth, he'll be angry.
- Unless you hurry up, you'll miss the train. (= If you don't hurry up...)
- If I were you, I'd talk to her about it.
- What would you do if you had a lot of money?

### Module 7: Great books
**核心语法**：被动语态综合（各种时态）
**重点句型**：
- The book was written by Mark Twain.
- It has been translated into many languages.
- The play is going to be performed next week.
- The story is set in the south of the United States.

**被动语态综合**：

| 时态 | 被动结构 |
|------|---------|
| 一般现在时 | am/is/are + done |
| 一般过去时 | was/were + done |
| 一般将来时 | will be + done |
| 现在完成时 | have/has been + done |
| 含情态动词 | can/should/must be + done |

### Module 8: Sports life
**核心语法**：被动语态（含情态动词）、make + 宾语 + 宾补
**重点句型**：
- The match can be watched live on TV.
- He was made to practise shooting every day.
- The decision made him very angry.
- She was encouraged to try again.

**make 用法总结**：
- make sb. do sth.（主动，使某人做某事）
- be made to do sth.（被动，被迫做某事）
- make sb./sth. + adj.（使…变得…）

### Module 9: Great inventions
**核心语法**：定语从句复习、被动语态复习
**重点句型**：
- Books which/that were written by hand were expensive.
- Paper was invented by the Chinese.
- Technology that has changed the world includes printing.
- The information that/which is stored on a computer can be shared.

### Module 10: Australia
**核心语法**：状语从句综合（时间/条件/原因/让步）
**重点句型**：
- Don't forget to bring your camera when you visit Australia.
- Although/Though it's far away, it's well worth a visit.
- Because it's surrounded by the ocean, the air is clean.
- You'll love it as soon as you see it.

### Module 11: Photos
**核心语法**：定语从句综合复习
**重点句型**：
- The photo which won the prize was taken by a 15-year-old boy.
- The person who took the photo was from Guangdong.
- I like photos that tell a story.

### Module 12: Save our world
**核心语法**：综合复习（被动语态+定语从句+状语从句）
**重点句型**：
- The rubbish that people throw away is polluting the earth.
- If everyone starts to care, the world will be saved.
- We should recycle things that can be used again.

## 下册

### Module 1: Travel
**核心语法**：综合时态复习、建议表达
**重点句型**：
- Have you ever been to a foreign country?
- You'd better bring a good map.
- I suggest (that) you visit the Great Wall.
- It was the most beautiful place I had ever seen.

### Module 2: Education
**核心语法**：过去完成时（had + 过去分词）
**重点句型**：
- By the time I got there, the train had already left.
- She had never been abroad before she went to Australia.
- After he had finished his homework, he went to bed.
- I realized I had left my keys at home.

**过去完成时标志**：by the time, before, after, when（表示"过去的过去"）

### Module 3: Life now and then
**核心语法**：过去完成时（续）、现在完成时对比
**重点句型**：
- Life has changed a lot since the 1960s.
- Before that, people had used horses for transport.
- By the end of last year, they had built three new hospitals.

### Module 4: Rules and suggestions
**核心语法**：情态动词综合（must/can/could/may/might/should/had better）
**重点句型**：
- You must be careful on the road.
- You might get lost if you don't take a map.
- Could you tell me the way to the library?
- You'd better not play with fire.

**情态动词表推测程度**：must(一定) > may/might/could(可能) > can't(不可能)

### Module 5: Look after yourself
**核心语法**：非谓语动词（动名词 / 不定式 / 分词）综合
**重点句型**：
- Running is good exercise for the body and the mind.
- I want to keep healthy.
- He suggested going for a walk every day.
- Keep smiling and be happy!

**接动名词(-ing)的动词**：enjoy, finish, practice, mind, keep, suggest, avoid, consider, imagine
**接不定式(to do)的动词**：want, hope, wish, decide, plan, agree, refuse, learn, choose, expect

### Module 6: Eating together
**核心语法**：宾语从句复习、感叹句
**重点句型**：
- Do you know where we can get some good food?
- What a delicious meal (it is)! / How delicious the food is!
- She said (that) the restaurant was the best in town.

**感叹句结构**：
- What + (a/an) + adj. + n. + (主+谓)!
- How + adj./adv. + (主+谓)!

### Module 7: English for you and me
**核心语法**：综合复习（从句+被动语态+非谓语）
**重点句型**：
- English is used by the largest number of people in the world.
- It's possible that we will all speak English one day.
- Learning a foreign language is one of the most difficult things.

### Module 8: My future life
**核心语法**：综合复习（所有语法点）
**重点句型**：
- I'm looking forward to going to senior high school.
- I'll never forget the friends that I have made here.
- Whatever happens, I'll keep trying.
- I hope our friendship will last forever.

## 中考语法总复习

### 八大时态综合对比

| 时态 | 结构 | 核心标志词 |
|------|------|----------|
| 一般现在时 | do/does | always, usually, every day, sometimes |
| 一般过去时 | did | yesterday, last..., ago, in+过去年份 |
| 一般将来时 | will do / be going to do | tomorrow, next..., in the future |
| 现在进行时 | am/is/are doing | now, look, listen, at the moment |
| 过去进行时 | was/were doing | at that time, when+过去时, while |
| 现在完成时 | have/has done | already, yet, ever, never, since, for, just |
| 过去完成时 | had done | by the time, before+过去时 |
| 过去将来时 | would do | (宾语从句中，主句为过去时) |

### 三大从句

| 从句 | 连接词 | 关键规则 |
|------|--------|---------|
| 宾语从句 | that / if(whether) / wh-词 | 陈述语序 + 时态呼应 |
| 定语从句 | who / which / that / whose | 关系代词代替先行词在从句中的成分 |
| 状语从句 | when/while/after/before/if/unless/although/because/so...that | if条件句：主将从现 |

### 被动语态综合

| 时态 | 被动结构 |
|------|---------|
| 一般现在时 | am/is/are + done |
| 一般过去时 | was/were + done |
| 一般将来时 | will be + done |
| 现在完成时 | have/has been + done |
| 含情态动词 | can/should/must be + done |

### 非谓语动词

| 形式 | 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| to do | 作主语/宾语/状语/定语/宾补 | I want to go. / It's important to study. |
| doing | 作主语/宾语/表语/定语/状语 | Swimming is fun. / I enjoy reading. |
| done | 作定语/宾补/表语 | a broken window / I found it broken. |

### 中考高频词组与句型

**常用短语**（按话题分类）：

学习类：work hard, be good at, do well in, catch up with, fall behind, pay attention to, look up, take notes

生活类：get along/on with, take care of, look after, help with, be interested in, be used to

社会类：take part in, make a contribution to, be proud of, set up, give up, depend on

**常用句型**：
- It's + adj. + (for sb.) + to do sth.
- It takes sb. + 时间 + to do sth.
- not...until... / not only...but also... / either...or... / neither...nor...
- so + adj./adv. + that... / such + (a/an) + adj. + n. + that...
- the + 比较级..., the + 比较级... (越…越…)

### 中考核心 1600 词（分级）

**A级核心词（约400词，必须掌握拼写和用法）**：
包括基本动词（be, have, do, go, come, get, make, take, give, see, know, think, say, tell, find, want, need, like, love, help, work, play, study, learn, read, write, speak, listen, eat, drink, sleep, run, walk, open, close, begin, stop, try, use...）和基本名词、形容词、副词等。

**B级重要词（约500词，需掌握词义和基本用法）**：
包括学科词汇、社会生活词汇、情感描述词汇等。

**C级认读词（约700词，需能在语境中理解）**：
包括阅读理解中可能出现的较低频词汇。
