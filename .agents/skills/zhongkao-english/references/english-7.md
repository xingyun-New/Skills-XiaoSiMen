# 七年级英语（上册 + 下册）

> 外研版（New Standard English）七年级英语教材知识点框架。待补充完整内容时，请参照 [textbook-guide.md](textbook-guide.md) 的格式添加。

## 上册

### Starter Module: My teacher and my friends
- 基本问候语：Hello / Hi / Good morning / Good afternoon / Good evening / Goodbye
- 自我介绍：My name is... / I'm... / Nice to meet you.
- 数字 1-100
- 26个英文字母

### Module 1: My classmates
**核心语法**：be动词（am/is/are）、形容词性物主代词
**重点句型**：
- I'm Chinese. I'm from China.
- Where are you from? — I'm from England.
- He/She is my friend. His/Her name is...
- Nice to meet you. — Nice to meet you, too.

**核心词汇**：Chinese, from, where, year, about, what, first name, last name

### Module 2: My family
**核心语法**：指示代词（this/that/these/those）、名词所有格
**重点句型**：
- This is my mother. / That is my father.
- These are my grandparents.
- Is this your sister? — Yes, it is. / No, it isn't.
- What's your father's job? — He's a manager.

**核心词汇**：family, parent, mother, father, sister, brother, photo, next to, in front of, behind

### Module 3: My school
**核心语法**：there be句型（肯定/否定/疑问）、方位介词
**重点句型**：
- There is a computer in my classroom.
- There are thirty students in my class.
- Is there a library? — Yes, there is. / No, there isn't.
- Are there any computers? — Yes, there are.

**核心词汇**：classroom, library, playground, office, building, dining hall, science lab, behind, next to, in front of

### Module 4: Healthy food
**核心语法**：have got / has got、可数与不可数名词、some/any
**重点句型**：
- Have you got any oranges? — Yes, I have. / No, I haven't.
- She has got some milk, but she hasn't got any juice.
- What food have you got?
- Too much chocolate isn't good for you.

**核心词汇**：food, drink, fruit, vegetable, meat, rice, noodle, chicken, juice, water, healthy, favourite

### Module 5: My school day
**核心语法**：一般现在时（肯定/否定/疑问，含三单变化）、时间表达
**重点句型**：
- I get up at seven o'clock.
- She goes to school at half past seven.
- We don't have Chinese on Monday.
- What time do you go to school? — At half past seven.
- Do you like maths? — Yes, I do. / No, I don't.

**一般现在时三单变化**：
- 一般加 -s：plays, likes, works
- s/x/ch/sh 结尾加 -es：watches, teaches, misses
- 辅音+y 结尾变i加 -es：studies, carries
- 特殊：have → has, go → goes, do → does

### Module 6: A trip to the zoo
**核心语法**：一般现在时（续）、频率副词
**重点句型**：
- The tiger lives in Asia.
- Does it eat meat? — Yes, it does.
- The panda doesn't eat grass.
- It usually sleeps during the day.

**核心词汇**：动物词汇（tiger, bear, elephant, monkey, panda, lion, giraffe, wolf, snake, camel）、eat, live, sleep, run, swim, fly, dangerous, cute, large

### Module 7: Computers
**核心语法**：一般现在时综合运用、连词 and/but/or
**重点句型**：
- I use the computer to do my homework.
- He often sends emails to his friends.
- We can search for information on the Internet.
- Do you use a computer at school? — Yes, we do.

**核心词汇**：computer, keyboard, mouse, screen, print, save, use, Internet, email, website, share, information

### Module 8: Choosing presents
**核心语法**：现在进行时（am/is/are + doing）
**重点句型**：
- What are you doing? — I'm buying a present.
- She is reading a book at the moment.
- Are they playing football? — Yes, they are. / No, they aren't.
- He isn't watching TV now.

**现在分词变化规则**：
- 一般加 -ing：play → playing, read → reading
- 去e加 -ing：write → writing, have → having, make → making
- 双写加 -ing：run → running, swim → swimming, sit → sitting, get → getting

### Module 9: People and places
**核心语法**：现在进行时（续，表正在进行的动作）
**重点句型**：
- They are having a good time.
- She is lying on the beach and enjoying the sun.
- People are visiting famous places.
- What are they doing right now?

**核心词汇**：enjoy, lie, take photos, postcard, wait for, drive, leave, get off, trip, travel

### Module 10: Spring Festival
**核心语法**：现在进行时表将来（be + doing 表计划/安排）
**重点句型**：
- We're getting ready for Spring Festival.
- What are you doing at the festival? — We're making lanterns.
- She is cleaning the house for the festival.

**核心词汇**：festival, Spring Festival, Christmas, lantern, firework, decoration, celebrate, lucky, tradition, dumpling

## 下册

### Module 1: Lost and found
**核心语法**：whose引导的特殊疑问句、名词性物主代词
**重点句型**：
- Whose bag is this? — It's mine/yours/his/hers.
- Is this your glove? — Yes, it is. / No, it isn't. Mine is blue.
- Are these your crayons? — No, they aren't. They are hers.

**核心词汇**：mine, yours, his, hers, ours, theirs, whose, lose, find, careful, crayon, glove, wallet, purple

### Module 2: What can you do?
**核心语法**：情态动词 can（肯定/否定/疑问）
**重点句型**：
- I can play the piano, but I can't play the guitar.
- Can you ride a horse? — Yes, I can. / No, I can't.
- What can she do? — She can dance very well.
- Don't worry about Chinese. I can help you.

**核心词汇**：play, ride, cook, dance, swim, speak, fly, drive, worry about, promise, teach

### Module 3: Making plans
**核心语法**：一般将来时（be going to + do）
**重点句型**：
- What are you going to do at the weekend?
- I'm going to check my email and do my homework.
- She is going to visit her grandparents on Saturday.
- Are you going to have a picnic? — Yes, we are.

**核心词汇**：plan, picnic, revise, holiday, sightseeing, forward, look forward to, enjoy oneself, take a walk

### Module 4: Life in the future
**核心语法**：一般将来时（will + do）
**重点句型**：
- Will there be schools in the future? — Maybe not.
- Everyone will study at home.
- Machines will do the heavy work.
- It won't be easy to find a job.

**核心词汇**：future, chalk, ruler, will, maybe, machine, robot, heavy, light, cheap, expensive, free, possible

### Module 5: Shopping
**核心语法**：would like的用法（would like to do / would like sth.）
**重点句型**：
- What would you like? — I'd like a T-shirt.
- What size would you like? — A medium, please.
- How much is it? — It's 198 yuan.
- Can I try it on? — Sure.

**核心词汇**：market, price, size, try on, pay, change, online, advantage, compare, product, receive, safe

### Module 6: Around town
**核心语法**：祈使句（肯定/否定）、问路与指路
**重点句型**：
- Could you tell me the way to the station?
- Go along the street and turn left at the third crossing.
- It's opposite the bank. / It's between the bookshop and the cinema.
- Is there a supermarket near here? — Yes. Go straight ahead.

**方位介词**：opposite, between...and, on the corner of, next to, near, behind

### Module 7: My past life
**核心语法**：be动词的过去式（was/were）
**重点句型**：
- I was born in a small town.
- Where were you born? — I was born in Beijing.
- She wasn't very friendly at first.
- Were you naughty as a child? — Yes, I was.

**核心词汇**：born, strict, friendly, naughty, primary school, bedroom, town, village, bathroom, garden, pond

### Module 8: Story time
**核心语法**：一般过去时（规则/不规则动词）
**重点句型**：
- Once upon a time, there was a little girl.
- She decided to go for a walk in the forest.
- He hurried to the house and knocked on the door.
- What happened next? — She picked up the basket and walked away.

**规则动词过去式变化**：
- 一般加 -ed：walked, played, visited
- 以e结尾加 -d：liked, lived, decided
- 辅音+y 变i加 -ed：studied, hurried, carried
- 双写加 -ed：stopped, planned

**高频不规则动词**：
go-went, do-did, have-had, eat-ate, see-saw, buy-bought, take-took, come-came, get-got, make-made, give-gave, read-read, write-wrote, sit-sat, run-ran, say-said, tell-told, begin-began, break-broke, bring-brought, know-knew, think-thought

### Module 9: Life history
**核心语法**：一般过去时（续）、when引导的时间状语从句
**重点句型**：
- When did he start school? — He started school in 1965.
- She became a teacher when she was twenty.
- Did he go to America? — Yes, he did. / No, he didn't.

**核心词汇**：marry, move, join, become, national, hero, rich, successful, poem, play (n.)

### Module 10: A holiday journey
**核心语法**：一般过去时综合运用
**重点句型**：
- Where did you go on holiday? — I went to Los Angeles.
- How did you get there? — We flew there.
- What did you do? — We went to Disneyland.
- The journey took about nine hours.

**核心词汇**：journey, flight, Pacific, arrive, relax, explore, special, experience, movie, actor, hope, wonderful

### Module 11: Body language
**核心语法**：祈使句复习、情态动词复习
**重点句型**：
- Don't stand too close to people.
- In some countries, you must touch people's heads.
- You should shake hands when you meet someone.

### Module 12: Western music
**核心语法**：一般过去时与一般现在时对比
**重点句型**：
- He was born in Austria and grew up in Europe.
- He is famous for his pop music.
- She gave concerts all over the world.
