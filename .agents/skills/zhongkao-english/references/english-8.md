# 八年级英语（上册 + 下册）

> 外研版（New Standard English）八年级英语教材知识点框架。待补充完整内容时，请参照 [textbook-guide.md](textbook-guide.md) 的格式添加。

## 上册

### Module 1: How to learn English
**核心语法**：提建议的表达（why don't you / why not / you should / how about）
**重点句型**：
- Why don't you write down your mistakes in your notebook?
- Why not try to find some English pen friends?
- You should speak English in class.
- How about listening to the radio?
- Let's try to speak English as much as possible.

**核心词汇**：translate, correct, match, number, repeat, grammar, pronunciation, spelling, advice, improve, basic, shy, conversation, quickly, natural

### Module 2: My home town and my country
**核心语法**：形容词/副词比较级
**重点句型**：
- It's taller than many other buildings.
- Beijing is bigger than Guangzhou.
- The population of Shanghai is larger than that of Shenzhen.
- Is it a newer city than Beijing? — Yes, it is.

**比较级变化规则**：
- 一般加 -er：tall → taller, old → older, small → smaller
- 以e结尾加 -r：nice → nicer, large → larger, wide → wider
- 辅音+y 变i加 -er：easy → easier, heavy → heavier, busy → busier
- 双写加 -er：big → bigger, thin → thinner, hot → hotter
- 多音节加 more：beautiful → more beautiful, important → more important
- 不规则：good/well → better, bad/badly → worse, many/much → more, little → less, far → farther/further

### Module 3: Sports
**核心语法**：形容词/副词比较级（续）、enough + n. / adj. + enough
**重点句型**：
- Nothing is more exciting than playing tennis.
- He trains harder than the other players.
- Running is cheaper than going to the gym.
- That's not enough training.

**核心词汇**：baseball, volleyball, tennis, table tennis, boring, exciting, relaxing, already, matter, hurt, enjoy, stadium, miss, mind, train, against, practice

### Module 4: Planes, ships and trains
**核心语法**：形容词/副词最高级
**重点句型**：
- What is the fastest way to travel?
- It's the most comfortable way, but it's also the most expensive.
- Which is the longest journey?
- He is the busiest man in the office.

**最高级变化规则**：与比较级对应，加 -est 或 most
- tall → tallest, nice → nicest, big → biggest, easy → easiest
- beautiful → most beautiful
- good/well → best, bad → worst, many/much → most

**最高级使用注意**：通常前面加 the，范围用 in/of

### Module 5: Lao She Teahouse
**核心语法**：动词不定式作宾语（want/decide/plan/hope/offer/agree to do sth.）
**重点句型**：
- I wanted to see the Beijing Opera.
- She offered to take us to the teahouse.
- Lao She decided to write a play about a teahouse.
- I hope to understand more next time.

**核心词汇**：offer, agree, describe, society, show, ending, magic, face, act, master, seat, audience

### Module 6: Animals in danger
**核心语法**：动词不定式作状语/定语、不定式的否定形式
**重点句型**：
- Many animals don't have a safe place to live.
- Let's find out what we can do to save them.
- We need to protect animals in danger.
- It's important not to buy things made from animals in danger.

**核心词汇**：snake, thin, danger, interested, allow, protect, grow, feed, take away, look after, set up, nature, research, produce, southwest

### Module 7: A famous story
**核心语法**：过去进行时（was/were + doing）
**重点句型**：
- What were you doing at nine o'clock last Sunday morning?
- I was sitting by the river and thinking about nothing.
- While she was looking at it, the Cheshire Cat appeared.
- Was she having her tea? — Yes, she was.

**过去进行时用法**：
- 表示过去某个时刻正在进行的动作
- 常与 at that time, at 9 o'clock, when, while 连用

### Module 8: Accidents
**核心语法**：过去进行时与一般过去时的搭配（when/while）
**重点句型**：
- When the accident happened, I was walking along the street.
- While I was waiting for the bus, the car hit a boy.
- What were you doing when it started to rain?
- She was crossing the road when a motorbike hit her.

**when vs. while**：
- when + 短暂动作（一般过去时）：When the phone rang, I was cooking.
- while + 持续动作（过去进行时）：While I was sleeping, the doorbell rang.

**核心词汇**：accident, appear, throw, fall, climb, hit, ride, push, attention, corner, direction, round

### Module 9: Population
**核心语法**：数字表达、大数读法、不定代词（something/anything/nothing/everyone）
**重点句型**：
- The population of China is about 1.4 billion.
- It's increasing by about 50 million every year.
- Is there anything else? — No, nothing else.
- Everyone should know about this problem.

**核心词汇**：billion, crowd, increase, noise, note, report, prepare, close, problem, protect, quiet, public, pollution, cause, solution

### Module 10: The weather
**核心语法**：情态动词 might（表推测）、compared to
**重点句型**：
- It might be cold tomorrow.
- You'd better take an umbrella.
- Compared to winter, summer is more pleasant.
- What's the weather like in spring? — It's warm and sunny.

**核心词汇**：cloud, shower, storm, degree, temperature, drop, awful, terrible, wet, dry, snowy, windy, cool, pleasant, probably, from time to time

### Module 11: Way of life
**核心语法**：情态动词 must / mustn't / have to / don't have to
**重点句型**：
- You must wait and open it later.
- You mustn't break anything during Spring Festival.
- Do I have to accept a present with both hands? — Yes, you do.
- You don't have to finish it all.

**must vs. have to**：
- must：主观意愿，"我必须"
- have to：客观需要，"不得不"
- mustn't：禁止（不能做）
- don't have to：不必（可以不做）

### Module 12: Help
**核心语法**：if引导的条件状语从句（主将从现）、祈使句 + and/or
**重点句型**：
- If you want to stay healthy, you should exercise more.
- If it rains tomorrow, we'll stay at home.
- Stay calm and everything will be OK.
- Hurry up, or you'll be late.

**主将从现规则**：主句用将来时（will do），if从句用一般现在时

## 下册

### Module 1: Feelings and impressions
**核心语法**：感官动词（look/smell/taste/sound/feel + adj.）
**重点句型**：
- It smells too strong and it tastes a bit sour.
- The music sounds beautiful.
- You look very pretty in that dress.
- I feel nervous before exams.

**感官动词用法**：后接形容词作表语，不接副词
- look + adj. = 看起来…
- sound + adj. = 听起来…
- smell + adj. = 闻起来…
- taste + adj. = 尝起来…
- feel + adj. = 感觉…

### Module 2: Experiences
**核心语法**：现在完成时（have/has + 过去分词）、already/yet/ever/never/just
**重点句型**：
- I've entered lots of speaking competitions.
- Have you ever won any prizes? — Yes, I have. / No, I haven't.
- She has already finished her homework.
- He hasn't arrived yet.
- I've just heard the good news.

**现在完成时标志词**：already（肯定句），yet（否定/疑问），ever（疑问），never（否定），just（刚刚）

### Module 3: Journey to space
**核心语法**：现在完成时（续）、have been to / have gone to
**重点句型**：
- Scientists have sent many rockets into space.
- Has anyone been to Mars? — No, not yet.
- Where has he gone? — He has gone to Shanghai.
- How many countries have you been to?

**三者区别**：
- have been to：去过（已回来）
- have gone to：去了（人不在这里）
- have been in：一直在（强调持续）

### Module 4: Seeing the doctor
**核心语法**：现在完成时（since/for 表示持续）、瞬间动词与延续性动词转换
**重点句型**：
- I've had a headache since this morning.
- She has been ill for three days.
- He has been a teacher since 2010.
- How long have you felt like this? — Since Monday.

**瞬间动词→延续性动词**：
- buy → have/keep
- borrow → keep
- join → be a member of / be in
- come/arrive → be in/at
- leave → be away from
- die → be dead
- open → be open
- close → be closed
- begin/start → be on

### Module 5: Cartoons
**核心语法**：现在完成时 vs. 一般过去时
**重点句型**：
- I've read that book — I read it last week.
- She has worked here for ten years. (持续到现在)
- She worked here ten years ago. (过去的事)
- Nemo has been popular since 2003.

**对比区别**：
- 现在完成时：强调过去的动作对现在的影响，或从过去延续到现在
- 一般过去时：强调过去某个时间发生的事，与现在无关

### Module 6: Hobbies
**核心语法**：简单句的五种基本句型、动词 + doing / to do 辨析
**重点句型**：
- My hobby is collecting stamps.
- I enjoy reading books about history.
- He always encourages me to try something new.
- Have you ever thought about starting a hobby?

**接动名词(-ing)的动词**：enjoy, finish, practice, mind, keep, suggest, avoid, consider
**接不定式(to do)的动词**：want, hope, wish, decide, plan, agree, refuse, learn, choose, expect, encourage

### Module 7: Summer in Los Angeles
**核心语法**：被动语态（一般现在时：am/is/are + done）
**重点句型**：
- English is spoken as a first language in the USA.
- Millions of tourists are attracted to Los Angeles every year.
- What language is spoken in Canada?
- The parade is watched by thousands of people.

### Module 8: Time off
**核心语法**：被动语态（一般过去时：was/were + done）
**重点句型**：
- The park was set up by the government in 1956.
- These animals were brought here from all over the world.
- When was the school built? — It was built in 2000.
- The bridge wasn't damaged in the storm.

### Module 9: Friendship
**核心语法**：宾语从句（that引导 / if-whether引导 / wh-词引导）
**重点句型**：
- I think (that) she is a very good friend.
- Could you tell me if/whether he is at home?
- Do you know what his phone number is?
- She asked me where I lived.

**宾语从句三要素**：
1. 连接词：that / if(whether) / wh-词
2. 语序：陈述语序（主+谓）
3. 时态：主句过去时→从句用相应过去时态（客观真理除外）

### Module 10: On the radio
**核心语法**：宾语从句（续）、间接引语初步
**重点句型**：
- He said that he wanted to be a radio presenter.
- She asked if I could help her.
- They told me that the show had started.
- I hope (that) you can come to the party.
